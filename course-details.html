Objectives

Embark on a transformative journey to become a skilled Full-Stack Web Developer. This comprehensive course is designed to equip you with both the front-end and back-end technologies needed to build, test, and deploy dynamic, database-driven web applications from the ground up. We provide a structured curriculum that takes you from the fundamentals of web design to advanced server-side concepts.

Our hands-on, project-based approach ensures that you not only learn the theory but also gain critical real-world experience. You will master essential technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React for the front-end, while diving deep into back-end development with Node.js, Express, and databases like MongoDB and SQL. By the end of this course, you will have built a robust portfolio of projects, giving you the confidence and the credentials to launch a successful career in the tech industry.

Eligibility

Our Full-Stack Web Developer course is designed for motivated individuals who are passionate about technology and eager to launch a career in software development. We welcome learners from all professional and academic backgrounds.

The primary requirements are a strong desire to learn, a problem-solving mindset, and a commitment to the program's schedule. You will also need access to a computer with a reliable internet connection.
